Welcome to the San Diego Model Railroad Association archive website. This site hosts descriptions, images, and historical notes linked to QR codes placed throughout the museum.

San Diego Model Railroad Assoc.

This model railroad club has two layouts in the San Diego Model Railroad Museum.

The first one is the Cabrillo & Southwestern which is a fictional O scale layout based on Southern California circa 1955. The layout has a 13 scale mile double track mainline, a narrow guage town, and mountatous scene.

The second one is the San Diego & Arizona Eastern RR wich is a realistic HO scale layout located on southeast California that heads into Mexico themed in the 1950s. The layout has 15 scale miles of mainline and follows the California-Mexico border before it crosses over into Mexito at Tecate and San Diego.
