About This Display

El Centro is the lowest point modeled on our layout. It is the beginning of the San Diego & Arizona Eastern route to San Diego. It is the location of out expertly modeled drive-in movie theater looping Roadrunner and Coyote cartoons. You can also spot the Southern Pacific and SD&AE depots on the outskirts of this bustling town.
